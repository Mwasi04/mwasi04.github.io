Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer free shipping within Kitwe for orders above K500. For other areas, we use reliable courier services with tracking.

Yes, all our products come with a minimum 12-month warranty. Extended warranties are available on selected items.

Can I return a product?

We have a 14-day return policy for unused products in original packaging. Visit our Returns & Refunds page for details.

Yes, we provide installation services for TVs, home theaters, and other electronics at an additional cost.
